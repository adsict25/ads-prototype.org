Our Partners

"Our partners have been instrumental in our past successes, contributing valuable resources, expertise, and unwavering support. Today, their collaboration continues to drive our growth and innovation, and together, we are poised to shape a promising future, creating lasting impact and fostering sustainable progress."

The Republic of Kenya

Collaboration with the Government of Kenya focuses on advancing health initiatives like universal health coverage, reproductive health programs, and early childhood development projects such as "Moments That Matter," strengthening community well-being and resilience.

Episcopal Relief & Development

The partnership with Episcopal Relief & Development enhances early childhood development, fosters gender equity through male caregiver engagement, and improves rural resilience with climate-adaptive agriculture

Kenya RedCross

The partnership with Kenya Red Cross under the Global Fund's CSS model combats HIV, TB, and malaria while improving maternal health through CHV training, strengthened health systems, and data-driven healthcare solutions.

The Primate's World Relief and Development Fund

ADS Nyanza, in partnership with PWRDF, enhances food security and livelihoods in Kenya by providing Galla goats to low-income, food-insecure families, fostering sustainable income generation through milk and meat sales, and promoting social inclusion and environmental sustainability

Bread for the World | Brot für die Welt

ADS Nyanza collaborates with Bread for the World to implement community development projects that focus on food security, gender equality, and climate resilience, empowering marginalized groups and improving livelihoods in rural areas.

Hilton Faundation

ADS Nyanza collaborates with the Hilton Foundation to implement programs that enhance health, education, and sustainable livelihoods in vulnerable rural communities.
